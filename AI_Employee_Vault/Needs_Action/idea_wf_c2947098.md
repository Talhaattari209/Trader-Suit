# Alpha Idea

1. Price breaks above the 30-minute opening range high

   - Breakout = close above the high of this range(last 12 candles)

2. Current bar body size  is at least 1.5x the average body size of the last 10 bars and it must be above mid point of last (londen) session

3. Price is above VWAP

4. Time is within first 2 hours of session (before 11:30 AM EST)
